Customer Churn Analysis

Exploring customer behaviour to understand why accounts leave — and what could keep them.

Overview

This project examined a customer dataset to identify the behaviours and attributes most associated with churn, then translated those findings into practical retention recommendations.

The problem

Customers were leaving, but it wasn't clear which signals predicted it or which segments were most at risk. Without that understanding, retention effort risked being spread thinly and ineffectively.

What I did

  • Cleaned and prepared the dataset using Python and Pandas.
  • Ran exploratory data analysis to compare churned vs. retained customers.
  • Used SQL to slice churn rates by tenure, product, and engagement.
  • Summarised the strongest churn drivers and at-risk segments.

Outcome

The analysis highlighted a small set of high-impact churn drivers and a clearly defined at-risk segment, giving a focused starting point for targeted retention campaigns.
