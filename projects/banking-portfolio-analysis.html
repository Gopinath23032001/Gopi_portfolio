Banking Portfolio Analysis

Turning CASA and deposit data into clear client segments and actionable cross-sell opportunities for branch teams.

Overview

Drawing on hands-on banking experience, this project analysed Current Account, Savings Account (CASA), and term-deposit portfolios to understand how balances were distributed across customer segments and branches. The goal was to help relationship managers prioritise outreach and grow the deposit book.

The problem

Portfolio reporting was fragmented across spreadsheets, making it hard to see which client segments were growing, which were dormant, and where product bundling could add value. Decisions relied on intuition rather than evidence.

What I did

  • Consolidated account-level data and cleaned it with Python and Pandas.
  • Wrote SQL queries to segment customers by balance, product mix, and activity.
  • Performed exploratory analysis to spot dormant accounts and cross-sell signals.
  • Built a Tableau dashboard tracking portfolio growth and segment health.

Outcome

The analysis produced a prioritised list of cross-sell opportunities and a repeatable dashboard, giving branch teams a single, evidence-based view of portfolio performance.
