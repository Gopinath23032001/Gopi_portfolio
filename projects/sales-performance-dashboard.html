Sales Performance Dashboard

A single interactive view of regional sales, margins, and team performance to guide weekly business decisions.

Overview

Built during my time managing B2B accounts for a mobiles and appliances distributor, this dashboard brought wholesale sales data into one place so leadership could track performance without waiting for manual reports.

The problem

Sales figures lived in scattered spreadsheets updated by different team members. By the time numbers were reconciled, the week was over — making it hard to react to underperforming regions or products in time.

What I did

  • Modelled the sales data and standardised it with Power Query and Excel.
  • Wrote SQL to aggregate sales by region, product category, and sales rep.
  • Designed Tableau views for revenue, margin, and target attainment.
  • Added filters so managers could drill into any region or time period.

Outcome

The dashboard replaced a slow manual reporting cycle with a live view, helping the team spot lagging regions early and reallocate effort to higher-margin products.
